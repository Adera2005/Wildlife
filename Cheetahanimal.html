One of the most recognizable animals in Kenya is the cheetah.These spotty cats-each with their own unique paterns -are skilled hunters,in part of their natural speed and agility.In fact, cheetahs are the fastest mammals on earth.They can accelarate from zero to 60 miles per hour in just three seconds. Another intresting cheetah fact? Unlike lions,tigers,jaguars,cheetahs can't roar-they can only purr.

These cats live and hunt in open grassland across Africa.They can be found in countries including Kenya,Algeria,South Africa and Niger

The International Union for the COnservation Nature(IUCN)classes cheetahs as vulnerable.This means the species is at high risk of extinction in the wild. There are just 6,517 mature cheetahs left in the wild,and their population is decreasing.Cheetahs are at particular risk from habitat loss because they need a large range.Most cheetahs live in unprotected areas,where they come into conflict with farmers
