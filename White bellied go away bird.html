The white-bellied go-away bird has a call sound like 'go-away'.It has a grey plumage,a white belly, and a huge crest on the top of its head.It also has white marks on its tail,which are visible when the bird is in flight.

White-belleid go-away birds live in the arid savannahs and woodlands of Kenya,Tanzania,Uganda,Sudan,South Sudan,Somalia and Ethopia.The IUCN lists this bird as least concern,with no major threats to its survival.
